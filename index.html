HARMONY

Designed and implemented by Dreamteam

Brief Overview

Visiting South Africa a tourist would not know what South African foods are, let alone what it pairs well with, Harmony solves this by allowing you to pull out your phone, take a picture of the food and its drink pairings will be presented to you, but it doesn't stop there, using continuous AI Harmony will provide the best possible pairings as it learns and develops over time.

Harmony is more than just an AI to identify food, we also have a social media aspect, allowing users to view pairings feed style, upvote, downvote or favourite them and also create pairings of their own. We also cater to businesses, they can use our recommendations system to view what pairings and tags are most likely to gain popularity and leverage this to create custom adverts that also appear to users within a custom radius of their shop.

Our Wow Factor

Collaborative Filtering and a Business feature add on.

Using collaborative filtering we can use other users interactions that are similar to your own to come up with a prediction for what you may like. To do this we used a K nearest neighbour algorithm due to its simplicity and because we use the most popular pairings when classifying our matrix.

Using the image lets consider Tim and John, both Tim and John like ice cream but since Tim also likes waffles and donuts, the system will recommend waffles and dounts to John.
We use this principle on a much larger scale to predict which pairings and tags will gain more popularity and market this to businesses who can now use these tags and pairings to create targeted ads to the user population, within a radius of their shop, this is done using geofencing, business users can specify the radius they wish to market the ads to, keeping in mind, the bigger the radius, the more expensive the ad will be. Doing this we allow Harmony to sustainably support itself without the need for external cash injection.
